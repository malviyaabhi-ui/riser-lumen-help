Quick Tour

How Riser Lumen works — from login to live screens in 8 steps

Step 1 of 8
Log in to Riser Lumen
Open app.riserlumen.com in any browser. Click the link in your invitation email to set your password, then sign in. You land directly on your property dashboard.
Works on Chrome, Edge, and Safari — no app download needed.

Step 2 of 8
Add a screen
Go to Screens and click Add screen. Give it a name — like "Lobby Left" or "F&B Menu Board". A 6-character pairing code is generated instantly.
Pairing codes expire after 24 hours. Generate a new one any time from the Screens page.

Step 3 of 8
Pair your display
On the physical screen, open the player URL in a browser or via LG URL Launcher. Enter the pairing code when prompted. The screen goes Online within 30 seconds.
Supports LG webOS, Samsung Tizen, Android 10+, and any browser-capable device.

Step 4 of 8
Upload your content
Go to Content and drag-and-drop images, videos, or HTML files into your library. Or paste a web URL. Everything is ready to add to any playlist immediately.
Recommended: 1920×1080px images. Max video size: 500MB. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, MP4, WebM, HTML5.

Step 5 of 8
Create a playlist
Go to Playlists and click New playlist. Add content items, set the duration for each one, and drag to reorder. Save — your playlist is ready to assign to any screen.
Create separate playlists per zone — Lobby, F&B, Gym. Mix images, videos, and web content.

Step 6 of 8
Assign to your screen
Click on a screen, select your playlist from the dropdown, and save. The screen updates within 30 seconds. Assign one playlist to an entire zone in one click.
Group screens into zones — Lobby, F&B, Gym — and bulk-assign content to all of them at once.

Step 7 of 8
Set a schedule
Use Schedule to automatically switch playlists by time of day or day of week — breakfast menu in the morning, dinner menu in the evening, weekend events on Fridays.
Schedules override the default playlist during their window, then return to it automatically when the window ends.
