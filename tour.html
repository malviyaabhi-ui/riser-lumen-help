Help Centre
Step 1 of 8
Log in to
Riser Lumen
Access your property dashboard from any browser, anywhere.
Open your invitation email and click the setup link
Set your password and sign in
You land directly on your Dashboard
Works on Chrome, Edge, and Safari — no install needed.
Step 2 of 8
Add a screen
Register your display before connecting it physically.
Go to Screens → Add screen
Give it a name — "Lobby Left", "F&B Menu Board"
A 6-character pairing code is generated instantly
Pairing codes expire after 24 hours. Generate a new one any time.
Step 3 of 8
Pair your display
Connect any physical screen to your account in under 60 seconds.
Open the player URL on the screen's browser
Enter the 6-character pairing code
Screen goes Online within 30 seconds
Supports LG webOS, Samsung Tizen, Android 10+, and any browser.
Step 4 of 8
Upload your content
Drag and drop files into your library — ready to use immediately.
Images: JPG, PNG, WebP — recommended 1920×1080
Video: MP4, WebM, MOV — up to 500MB
Web URLs, HTML5 widgets, or ready-made Templates
Organise into folders by zone — Lobby, F&B, Gym.
Step 5 of 8
Create a playlist
Build an ordered loop of content that plays on repeat.
Go to Playlists → New playlist
Add content items, set duration for each
Drag to reorder, then save
Create separate playlists per zone — Lobby, F&B, Gym.
Step 6 of 8
Assign to screen
Link your playlist to one screen or an entire zone in one click.
Click a screen in the Screens list
Select your playlist from the dropdown
Screen updates within 30 seconds
Assign to an entire zone at once from the Groups panel.
Step 7 of 8
Set a schedule
Automatically switch playlists by time of day or day of week.
Breakfast menu 7am–11am, dinner menu 6pm–10pm
Weekend event playlist on Fridays and Saturdays
Screens return to default playlist automatically
Schedules override and restore the default — no manual switching.
Step 8 of 8
You're live 🎉
Your screens are playing. The Dashboard shows everything in real time.
Real-time screen health and online/offline status
Property intelligence score out of 100
Active campaigns, alerts, and revenue
Next: invite your team, run ad campaigns, view Analytics.
